Welcome to Moontube

How to Join the Moontube Platform

Step 1: Visit the Moontube Website

demo.moontube.io

Step 2: Create an Account

Click on the "Login" button at the top-right corner of the homepage.

Enter your details

Verify your email address through our custom confirmation link sent to your inbox.

Step 3: Set Up Your Profile

Once logged in, you’ll be prompted to complete your profile.

Add your bio, profile picture, and social media links.

Specify your role (Developer, Influencer, Investor) to access the relevant features.

Step 4: Explore the Dashboard

After completing your profile, you’ll be directed to the dashboard where you can see the platform's features.
